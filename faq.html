Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our jewelry and custom services

Are your gemstones natural?

Yes. We work with carefully selected natural gemstones. Each stone may show unique characteristics, which are part of its natural beauty.

Do you offer custom-made jewelry?

Absolutely. We specialize in bespoke and made-to-order pieces. You may contact us to discuss design ideas, materials, and budget.

How long does production take?

Production time typically ranges from 7–14 business days, depending on design complexity and material availability.

Will the finished piece look exactly like the photos?

As each piece is handcrafted and uses natural materials, slight variations may occur. These differences make every piece unique.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer worldwide shipping. Shipping options and timelines will be confirmed during the order process.

How can I enquire about pricing?

You may contact us via email or WhatsApp. Pricing is confirmed individually based on design, materials, and customization requirements.
