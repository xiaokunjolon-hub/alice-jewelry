Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or contact us about custom jewelry.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, and message when you contact us.
  • Any information you share related to a custom order request (e.g., size, materials, design preferences).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide customer support.
  • Communicate about custom designs, quotes, production status, and delivery arrangements.
  • Improve our website experience and service quality.

3. Cookies & Basic Analytics

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics to help us understand site usage and improve performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information only when necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations.
  • Protect our rights and prevent fraud or misuse.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. To make a request, contact us at: xiaokunjolon@gmail.com.
