Shipping & Returns

Last updated: December 2025

Worldwide Shipping Tracking Where Available Secure Packaging

1. Production Time

Many pieces are made to order. Typical production time is 7–14 business days, depending on design complexity and material availability. We will confirm an estimated timeline during your enquiry.

2. Shipping

  • We ship internationally. Shipping options and costs are confirmed during order arrangement.
  • Tracking is provided where supported by the courier.
  • Delivery time varies by destination and customs processing.

3. Customs & Duties

International shipments may be subject to customs duties, taxes, or import fees. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient.

4. Returns & Exchanges

  • Custom-made items: generally non-refundable due to bespoke nature.
  • Non-custom items (if applicable): returns may be accepted within 7 days of delivery, provided items are unused and in original condition.

5. How to Request a Return

Please contact us before returning any item. Email xiaokunjolon@gmail.com or WhatsApp +86 13812904265 with your order details and reason for return request.
